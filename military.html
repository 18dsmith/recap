Military

Dominicut focuses the majority of its spending on it's military. It is also compulsory for all those aged over 18 to take part in at least 2 years of military service. Because of this, Dominicut has one of the, if not the most manpower in WakaNations. The military also has a variety of Virtual Reality tech, designed to help make training soldiers and pilots a lot cheaper, and more fun. This tech comes from the research undertaken between Dominicut and Bungquat. The list of planes currently in service in Dominicut can be found here.
